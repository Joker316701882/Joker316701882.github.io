Zheng Ge

Researcher at Megvii Inc., Beijing, China.

He currentlty works as a researcher at Megvii Technology, supervised by Dr.Xiangyu Zhang. He leads a team working on unified foundation models for autonomous driving, as well as 2D detection models on solving real-world challenges. His team develops and maintains several well-known projects, including YOLOX and BEVDepth series.

He obtained his Ph.D degree in Computer Science at Waseda University, supervised by Prof.Osamu Yoshie in 2022. He got his Bachelor degree in ECE department of Southeast University in 2017.

He is also interested in Multi-modal Foundation Models, AIGC and Self-supervised Representation Learning.
